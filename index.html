ActPass · actpass.org

Stop risky agent actions before they execute.

As organizations hand real authority to AI agents, "trust me" is not a control. ActPass sits between your agents and their tools and turns every consequential action into a deterministic, signed decision — with a paper trail auditors can actually verify.

Deterministic policy, capability by capability

Define exactly what an agent may do in each system — the same policy the SDK enforces at runtime.

Blast-radius limits

Quantitative guardrails — max spend per transaction, rate limits per hour — contain damage even when an agent misbehaves.

Tamper-evident evidence chain

Every allow, deny, or approval is issued as a signed Action Passport and sealed into a hash-chained ledger — replay-proof and verifiable.

Research · with Fannin

AI-driven raptamer discovery for the life sciences.

Raptamers™ are next-generation aptamers — DNA molecules engineered with protein-like modifications for higher affinity and more specific target binding. In partnership with Fannin, we apply modern machine learning to compress the discovery cycle: learning the sequence-to-binding landscape to prioritize candidates against proteins, small molecules, and whole cells.

Model-guided candidate selection

ML models rank and design binders, focusing wet-lab effort on the sequences most likely to succeed.

Therapeutic & diagnostic reach

Raptamers span therapeutics, diagnostics, and research tools — a versatile modality across disease areas.

A learning loop

Every assay result feeds back into the models, compounding discovery advantage over successive rounds.
